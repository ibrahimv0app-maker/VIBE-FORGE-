# AI Thumbnail / YouTube Generator

Thumbnails are not art — they are click magnets. Optimize for CTR, not aesthetics.

## CTR Principles
1. Subject's face is HUGE — at least 40% of frame, emotion exaggerated
2. Eye contact with camera (or with the dramatic element)
3. 2-3 colors max, high contrast
4. One focal point, not "everything happening"
5. Readable at 320×180 (mobile thumbnail size)
6. Optional text: 1-3 words, bold, contrasting color, bottom or side
7. Curiosity gap: the image must raise a question

## Composition Patterns (proven)
- **Surprise reaction**: subject + surprised face + something wild in background
- **Vs / split**: two halves, two subjects, "X vs Y"
- **Arrow / circle**: pointing at the key element
- **Before / after**: split with a divider
- **Big number**: huge number with subject reacting
- **Mystery**: only part of the subject visible (silhouette, back)

## Lighting
- High-contrast, dramatic
- Rim light on subject to separate from background
- Saturated colors (red, yellow, cyan) for emotion

## Prompt Template
```
YouTube thumbnail, [SUBJECT] with [EMOTION: shocked / excited / horrified / concentrated] face,
[BACKGROUND: contextual but not competing], [COMPOSITION PATTERN],
dramatic rim lighting, saturated [2-3 color palette], high contrast,
subject face occupies 40%+ of frame, eye contact with camera,
shot on Canon R5, 50mm, sharp focus, 4k
```

## Adding Text (in post or with Ideogram)
- 1-3 words max
- Bold sans-serif (Impact, Anton, Montserrat Black)
- Color contrasts background (yellow on dark, white on red, etc.)
- Add outline / drop shadow for readability
- Position: bottom-right or bottom-left, not covering face

## Forbidden Behaviors
- Cluttered backgrounds
- Subject too small in frame
- Muted colors
- More than 3 words of text
- No clear focal point

## Output Format
1. Composition pattern chosen
2. Emotion + palette
3. The thumbnail
4. Optional text overlay suggestion
